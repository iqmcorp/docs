# HubSpot Integration

Create matched audiences using HubSpot contact lists to enhance your targeting capabilities in IQM.

## Prerequisites

Before using the HubSpot integration, you must have:
- An active IQM account
- A HubSpot account with contact lists

## Install and Authorize HubSpot

### Step 1: Install the HubSpot App

1. Visit **Marketplace Apps** on the IQM Platform
2. Select the **HubSpot** app
3. Open the HubSpot app and click the **Install** button

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step1Marketplace} /></center>
<center><img className='diagram' src={Step1Install} /></center>



### Step 2: Access the App

Once the app is installed, access it from:
- The home page of available apps, OR
- The 9 dots icon in the top right of the header (HubSpot will appear at the bottom of the list)

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step2Install} /></center>

### Step 3: Authorize IQM Access

1. Click the **Authorize App** button in the app overview page
2. Select your HubSpot account
3. Authorize IQM access with the HubSpot account

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step3Authorize} /></center>

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step4HubspotLogin} /></center>

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step5Confirm} /></center>

:::info[note]
If your HubSpot account is not logged in, go to [HubSpot<ExternalLink />](https://hubspot.com) and log in first, then return to authorize.
:::

## Create a Matched Audience Using HubSpot Contact List

### Step 1: Open the Audience App

Go to the **Audience** app from the top right corner of the platform.

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step7NewAudience} /></center>

### Step 2: Create Matched Audience

1. Click the **Create Matched Audience** button
2. Select **Import from HubSpot** option

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step2Import} /></center>

### Step 3: Select Contact List

1. Select the **hubspot-contact-file name** from the contact lists dropdown
2. Download the data

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step8HubspotTab} /></center>

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step9AudienceName} /></center>

### Step 4: Map Columns

1. Match the columns with the uploaded file column names
2. Click **Create** to create the matched audience from the HubSpot contact list

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step10SelectList} /></center>

For more information about matching criteria, see [Selecting a Matched Audience<ExternalLink />](https://help.iqm.com/en/articles/5651480-selecting-a-matched-audience).

## Installing for Additional Users

Any other user within your organization must also install the HubSpot app within their instance of IQM's Platform:

1. Each user selects the HubSpot app from the Marketplace Apps
2. User clicks the **Request Admin** button to send a request for the app

<center><img className='diagram' src={ReqAdmin} /></center>

Users will be notified whenever an app is added into the platform ecosystem via the Intercom icon and the 9 dots icon in the header.

## FAQs

### What do I do if I face connection issues?

If the connected app faces connection issues, you can re-establish the connection by clicking the **Re-establish Connection** button.

<center><img className='diagram' src={Reconnect} /></center>

### How do I revoke users from accessing HubSpot?

1. Go to **Marketplace Apps** and click on the HubSpot app
2. Click on the **User Avatars** to open the user list with app access
3. De-select the user you would like to revoke access for
4. Click **Save Changes** to complete the action

<center><img className='diagram' src={FaqUsers} /></center>

### How do I revoke my access to HubSpot?

You can revoke access from two locations:

**From the App Marketplace:**
1. Enter into the HubSpot App Overview Page
2. Click on **Revoke access**

**From "My Profile" page:**
1. Enter into My Profile in IQM's Platform
2. Select HubSpot
3. Click on **Revoke access**

Once revoked, users need to integrate the app again to use it.

<center><img className='diagram' src={FaqManage} /></center>

### How do I uninstall the HubSpot app?

Click **Uninstall** in the Marketplace Apps to remove the application from the platform.

<center><img className='diagram' src={Uninstall} /></center>

### How do I switch to another HubSpot account?

To switch HubSpot accounts:
1. First **Log out** from your current account in the Marketplace App Overview page
2. Then integrate with another HubSpot account

<center><img className='diagram' src={FaqInstall} /></center>

## Use Cases

- **Retargeting Campaigns**: Target existing customers from your HubSpot CRM
- **Lookalike audiences**: Build lookalike audiences based on your best customers
- **Exclusion lists**: Exclude existing customers from prospecting Campaigns
- **Cross-sell Campaigns**: Target specific customer segments for upsell opportunities
