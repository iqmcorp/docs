# Google Sheets Integration

Pull IQM reporting data directly into Google Sheets for easy data analysis and collaboration.

## Prerequisites

Before using the IQM Reports Google Sheets integration, you must have:
- An active IQM account with API access
- A Google account with access to Google Sheets

## Install the Add-on

1. Visit the [IQM Google Workspace Marketplace listing](https://workspace.google.com/marketplace/app/iqm_%E2%80%94_vertical_specific_advertising/311816388465)
2. Click **Install** to add the IQM add-on to your Google account
3. Follow the prompts to authorize the installation

## Authorize Your IQM Account

### Step 1: Open the Add-on

1. Open a Google Spreadsheet
2. Navigate to **Extensions > IQM — Vertical Specific Advertising > Launch**

<center><img className='diagram' src={ExtensionsMenu} style={{width: '50%'}} /></center>

:::info[note]
The add-on is located in the **Extensions** menu at the top of Google Sheets. If you don't see "IQM — Vertical Specific Advertising" listed, make sure you've installed the add-on from the [Google Workspace Marketplace](https://workspace.google.com/marketplace/app/iqm_%E2%80%94_vertical_specific_advertising/311816388465).
:::

### Step 2: Select Your Google Account

Select your Google account in the "Choose an Account" popup.

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step2Authorization} style={{width: '50%'}}/></center>

### Step 3: Grant Access

When prompted, click **Allow** to allow the connector to connect to your Google account.

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step3Credentials} style={{width: '50%'}}/> <img className='diagram' src={Step4GenerateReport} style={{width: '50%'}} /></center>

## Generate IQM Reports

### Step 1: Launch the Integration

1. Navigate to **Extensions > IQM — Vertical Specific Advertising > Launch**

<center><img className='diagram' src={ExtensionsMenu} style={{width: '50%'}} /></center>

### Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

1. Under **Credentials**, enter your IQM account credentials (email and password)
2. Click **Login**

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step6Output} style={{width: '75%'}} /></center>

### Step 3: Select Customer

Select the Customer for which you want to generate reports.

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step7Dimensions} style={{width: '75%'}} /></center>

### Step 4: Configure Report Parameters

Select your **Parameters** and then click **Generate Report**:
- **Timezone**: The timezone for date interpretation
- **Start Date**: Beginning of the reporting period
- **End Date**: End of the reporting period
- **Dimensions**: How data should be grouped (Campaigns, Creatives, etc.)
- **Metrics**: The performance metrics to include (Impressions, Clicks, CTR, etc.)

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step8Metrics} /></center>

## Key Features

- **Real-time data sync**: Pull live Campaign data directly into your spreadsheets
- **Custom queries**: Filter and select specific metrics and dimensions
- **Collaborative analysis**: Share insights with team members in real-time
- **Flexible reporting**: Choose your own dimensions and metrics for custom reports
