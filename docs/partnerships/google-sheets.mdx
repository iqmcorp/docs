# Google Sheets Integration

Pull IQM reporting data directly into Google Sheets for easy data analysis and collaboration.

## Prerequisites

Before using the IQM Reports Google Sheets integration, you must have:
- An active IQM account with API access
- A Google account with access to Google Sheets

## Authorize Your IQM Account

### Step 1: Open the Google Spreadsheet App

1. Open the Google Spreadsheet App
2. Click on **IQM Reports** and select **Launch**

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step1Addon} /></center>

### Step 2: Select Your Google Account

Select your Google account in the "Choose an Account" popup.

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step2Authorization} /></center>

### Step 3: Grant Access

When prompted, click **Allow** to allow the connector to connect to your Google account.

## Generate IQM Reports

### Step 1: Launch the Integration

1. Click on **IQM Reports** and select **Launch**

### Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

1. Under **Credentials**, enter your IQM account credentials (email and password)
2. Click **Login**

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step3Credentials} /></center>

### Step 3: Select Customer

Select the Customer for which you want to generate reports.

### Step 4: Configure Report Parameters

Under **Parameters**, select:
- **Timezone**: The timezone for date interpretation
- **Start Date**: Beginning of the reporting period
- **End Date**: End of the reporting period
- **Dimensions**: How data should be grouped (Campaigns, Creatives, etc.)
- **Metrics**: The performance metrics to include (Impressions, Clicks, CTR, etc.)

## Key Features

- **Real-time data sync**: Pull live Campaign data directly into your spreadsheets
- **Custom queries**: Filter and select specific metrics and dimensions
- **Collaborative analysis**: Share insights with team members in real-time
- **Flexible reporting**: Choose your own dimensions and metrics for custom reports
