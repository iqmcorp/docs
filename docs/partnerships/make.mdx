## Key Features

- **Visual workflow builder**: Create automations with drag-and-drop simplicity
- **Multi-app integrations**: Connect IQM with thousands of other applications
- **Conditional logic**: Build complex workflows with branching and filters
- **Scheduled runs**: Automate data syncs and reports on a schedule

## Use Cases

- **Automated reporting**: Send campaign reports to Slack, email, or other channels
- **Data sync**: Keep your data warehouse updated with IQM campaign data
- **Alert notifications**: Get notified when campaigns reach specific thresholds
- **Cross-platform workflows**: Connect IQM data with your marketing stack
