# Improvado Integration

Improvado helps marketing teams find answers in data effortlessly. The IQM Connector allows users to integrate IQM ad-serving data into their Improvado dashboards, enhancing decision-making and connecting marketing, sales, and financial data.

## Prerequisites

Before using the IQM connector on Improvado, you must have:
- An active IQM account
- An Improvado account (see [improvado.io](https://improvado.io/) for information on gaining access)

## Setup

### Step 1: Log In to Improvado

Log into your Improvado account at [https://report.improvado.io/login/](https://report.improvado.io/login/).

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step1Login} /></center>

### Step 2: Create New Connection

1. From the **Extract & Load** sidebar, select **Connections**
2. Select **Make a new connection**

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step2Connections} /></center>

### Step 3: Select IQM Data Source

From the **Data sources** list, select **IQM**. This will open the Make a new connection wizard.

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step3IqmSource} /></center>

### Step 4: Authorize

1. Enter your details in the form
2. Press **Authorize**

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step4Authorize} /></center>

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step5Connected} /></center>

:::info[note]
You can request Client ID & Secret from [support@iqm.com](mailto:support@iqm.com).
:::

### Step 5: Extract Data

1. In the list of **Connected Sources**, press the **Extract** link for IQM Data Source
2. In the list of **Accounts**, select your IQM account and press **Continue**

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step6Extract} /></center>

### Step 6: Choose Template

Choose your desired template from the list of **Templates** and press **Continue**.

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step7Template} /></center>

### Step 7: Configure Extraction

Configure your extraction template by specifying:

**Schedule:**
- Set when and how often data should be extracted

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step8Configure} /></center>

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step9Schedule} /></center>

**Dimensions:**
- Select how data should be grouped (Campaigns, Creatives, etc.)

**Metrics:**
- Select which performance metrics to include

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step10Dimensions} /></center>

Press **Continue** after configuring.

### Step 8: Complete Extraction

1. Press **Continue** on the Result extraction orders step
2. Wait for your Extraction order to complete syncing

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step11Complete} /></center>

### Step 9: Explore Your Data

1. Navigate to **Data explorer** in the Extract & Load side menu
2. Select your extraction order in the **Extraction orders** column
3. Select the **Schema Preview** tab in Data explorer to explore your data preview

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step12DataExplorer} /></center>

<center><img className='diagram' src={Step13Preview} /></center>

## Key Features

- **Automated data extraction**: Pull IQM Campaign data automatically
- **Data transformation**: Clean and normalize data for analysis
- **AI-powered insights**: Leverage Improvado's AI for optimization recommendations
- **Multi-source reporting**: Combine IQM data with other marketing channels
