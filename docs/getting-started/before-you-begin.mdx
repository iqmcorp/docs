# Before You Begin

In order to use the API's service, you must have the following:

* A Client ID and Client Secret
* An account on the IQM platform
* At least one active Campaign


Follow these steps to meet the above conditions:

1. Email [support@iqm.com<ExternalLink />](mailto:support@iqm.com) to Request a Client ID and Client Secret. 
1. [Sign Up](/quickstart-guides/authentication-quickstart-guide) for an IQM Account:
   * Use a work email as a username and create a secure password
     * Password must be at least 8 characters in length
     * Requires at least 1 uppercase letter
     * Requires at least 1 lowercase letter
     * Requires at least 1 special character
1. To Run a Campaign:
   * See the [Create a Campaign](/quickstart-guides/create-a-campaign-quickstart) Quickstart Guide
